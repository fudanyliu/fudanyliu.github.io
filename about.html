About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at Tongji University, specializing in artificial intelligence and multimodal signal processing. My research focuses on developing innovative AI solutions for smart industry and autonomous driving applications.

With a academic background from Fudan University and University of Toronto, I am passionate about advancing the frontiers of AI research while ensuring practical real-world impact.
